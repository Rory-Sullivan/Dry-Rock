Dry Rock

Ever found yourself clicking through forecast after forecast trying to figure out where to go climbing at the weekend? Well then you've come to the right place!

Dry Rock is designed to be a one stop shop for figuring out where to go climbing.

  • Select your region from the list on the top left, and select your preferred units from the dropdown on the top right.
  • The overview table for each region has a 7-day rainfall summary for popular climbing destinations.
  • Weather values are given colour codes to indicate how good/bad they are for climbing; good, okay, or bad.
  • Clicking on a row in the tables will allow you to drill into more detail for that place and day.

Is there an app?

There is not an app but if you go to the page relevant to you on your mobile web browser, hit the three dots on the top bar and select "save to home screen" a link to the site will appear on your home screen.
