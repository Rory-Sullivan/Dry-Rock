About

Ever found yourself clicking through seemingly endless forecasts trying to figure out where to go climbing at the weekend? Well then you've come to the right place!

Dry Rock is designed to be a one stop shop for figuring out where to go climbing.

  • The overview table has a seven day rainfall summary for popular climbing destinations in Ireland
  • Low rainfall values are shown in green to make them easier to spot
  • Clicking a row in the overview table will take you to a 7-day forecast for that place
  • Clicking a row in the 7-day forecast table will show you a pop up with an hourly (or 6-hourly) detailed forecast for that day
  • Clicking the overiew button will take you back to the overview table
